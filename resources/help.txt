Commands:
  avg                                   calculate average duration of a call
  max spender                           show the name of the employee who spent the most on the calls
  calls from DD.MM.YYYY to DD.MM.YYYY   show all calls that took place in the given period of time
  total from DD.MM.YYYY to DD.MM.YYYY   calculate total cost of all calls that took place in the given period of time
  number FIRST_NAME LAST_NAME           get number of the given employee
  calls cost from DOUBLE                show all calls with costs that is more or equal
  calls duration from INT               show all calls with duration that is more or equal
  help                                  show this help message
  stop                                  stop the session
